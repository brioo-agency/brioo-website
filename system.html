Common questions

No BS. Just what you need to know.

How do you guarantee real engagement?+
We don't guarantee results — that would be a lie. What we do is manually audit every creator: comments, shares, saves, audience geography, and past brand work. If we find bots or fakes, we reject them.
What if the campaign flops?+
Then we don't get paid. Our commission is tied to successful delivery. We are as invested in the campaign working as you are.
Do you have case studies?+
We are a new agency (founded 2026). We don't have past results to show. What we have is a clear, honest process and the willingness to prove ourselves one deal at a time.
How much does it cost?+
15‑20% commission on each deal. You pay only when the campaign is delivered. No retainers, no hidden fees.
Do you take a cut before I get paid?+
No. You get your full agreed rate. We collect our commission from the brand after you are paid.
What if I have only 5,000 followers?+
We care about engagement, not follower count. Many micro‑creators with loyal audiences outperform mega‑influencers.
What kind of brands will I work with?+
Authentic, values‑aligned brands that respect creative freedom. No shady deals, no “exposure” scams.
How do I apply?+
Email creators@brioo.org with your social handles and engagement screenshots — we'll respond within 72 hours.
