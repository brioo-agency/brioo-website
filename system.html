Our System

A process built to
eliminate waste.

Every step is designed to protect both the creator and the brand. Research-first. Transparent throughout. Built for the long term.

01
Phase one
Research & Vetting

We audit before we ever reach out.

Before a single message is sent, we put every potential creator through a rigorous vetting process. Follower count is the last thing we look at.

Engagement audit

We analyze real engagement rate — not just likes, but saves, shares, comments quality, and story interactions.

Audience analysis

Demographics, geo-distribution, and audience quality scores. We look for real humans, not inflated reach.

Brand alignment check

Does this creator's content, tone, and values naturally align with the brand? If it feels forced, we move on.

Ethical filter

We only work in halal, values-aligned niches. Any creator or brand outside those lines doesn't make the list.

02
Phase two
Strategic Outreach

Human-first. No templates.

Every outreach message is personal. We reference specific content, explain why the match makes sense, and make it easy to say yes — or no. Creators respect that.

Personalized messaging

Every outreach references specific content from the creator. We don't send mass DMs. Every message takes time.

Multi-channel approach

Email + Instagram DM. We follow up once — professionally and clearly. No spam, no pressure.

Clear value proposition

We explain the brand, the deal structure, and what we're asking for upfront. Creators know exactly what they're getting into.

Systematic tracking

Every outreach is logged, tracked, and followed up on schedule. Nothing falls through the cracks.

03
Phase three
Negotiation & Management

We protect both sides.

Once there's interest, we handle everything — contract terms, content guidelines, payment structure, and deliverable deadlines. You don't chase anyone.

Contract drafting

Clear agreements that protect the creator's creative autonomy and the brand's expectations. No ambiguity.

Rate negotiation

We negotiate fair rates based on real engagement data — not inflated industry standards or underselling creators.

Deliverable tracking

Content briefs, deadlines, revision rounds — we manage the full workflow so brands get what they paid for.

Payment handling

We ensure creators get paid on time and brands get confirmation of delivery. Clean, transparent, no disputes.

Common questions

Everything you need to know.

If your question isn't here, email us — we reply same day.

We use a combination of manual analysis and third-party tools to check follower growth patterns, engagement rate vs. follower count ratios, comment quality, and audience geography. An account with 300K followers but 0.2% engagement and comments like "Great post! 🔥" fails our filter. We look for creators whose audience is genuinely invested in their content.
We focus on halal and values-aligned niches: Islamic content, self-improvement, personal finance, business and entrepreneurship, education, and fitness. We don't work with gambling, alcohol, dating apps, explicit content, or anything that conflicts with our ethical standards. This isn't a limitation — it's a feature. Brands in our niches benefit from highly engaged, trust-oriented audiences.
Brioo earns a 15–20% commission on each deal we close. There's no upfront retainer, no agency fee, and no charges if a campaign doesn't get placed. Our incentives are perfectly aligned with yours — we only earn when you do. Commission is calculated on the total deal value and collected after payment is confirmed by the brand.
We primarily work with creators in the 200K–500K range — mid-tier influencers who have significant reach but are often overlooked by large agencies. This range consistently delivers the best engagement-to-reach ratio. We're selective within this range and only approach creators whose engagement we'd be proud to show a brand.
Outreach and initial conversations typically take 2–4 weeks to generate responses and interest. Contract finalization and content delivery then follows. We don't promise overnight results because good partnerships take a little time to build. What we can promise is daily outreach, weekly updates, and complete transparency on what's working and what isn't.
