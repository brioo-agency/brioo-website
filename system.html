The Brioo System

How we turn
trust into deals.

Most agencies talk strategy. Brioo runs a specific, repeatable system — built from the ground up to serve creators who deserve better deals and brands who need to reach audiences that actually convert.

The Playbook

Five phases.
One clear direction.

This isn't a vague approach — it's a structured playbook that moves from first contact to closed deal. Every phase has a specific function, and nothing moves until the previous step is right. This is how we protect creators, serve brands, and run a business built on trust.

Phase One01

Foundation

Before any creator or brand is contacted, the agency infrastructure is built: identity, website, legal structure, outreach templates, and tracking systems. Nothing moves without this base. Credibility is earned before it's claimed.

Brands and creators who research us need to find a professional, values-aligned operation — not a hustle with a Gmail address.

Website & BrandingEmail SetupCreator TrackerBrand Pipeline CRMCalendly IntegrationContract Templates
Phase Two02

Outreach

Consistent, high-quality outreach to creators and brands. Every message is personal, short, and natural — written like a human, not a marketing funnel. For creators, we lead with the pain: brands are already looking for their audience, money is being left on the table, and Brioo is the connection they're missing.

Creator identities are never shared with brands during outreach. Audience demographics and ROI are pitched — names, channels, and locations stay private until budget is confirmed.

Personalised outreachBlind pitchingIdentity protectionPipeline trackingPain-first messaging
Phase Three03

Deals

When a brand confirms budget interest, the creator is introduced. From that point, we handle everything: rate discussions, content deliverables, usage rights, timelines, and contract drafting. Both parties sign via DocuSign or PandaDoc.

Payment goes through Brioo or directly to the creator depending on deal structure. Our commission (15–20%) is deducted only after the creator is paid — never before. Payout window is 15 days from receipt.

Rate negotiationContract via DocuSignPayment tracking15-day payout windowBrand vetting
Phase Four04

Systems

Every creator contact and brand outreach is tracked. Google Drive sheets are updated after every interaction — status, date, subject, and notes. Every signed deal triggers a contract folder and payment tracker. This infrastructure means nothing falls through the cracks as the roster grows.

Google Drive syncCreator TrackerBrand PipelineContract foldersPayment tracking
Phase Five05

Scale

Once the first milestone is achieved — consistent closed deals, satisfied creators, repeat brand partners — the roster expands. Long-term retainer partnerships are pursued. Multi-creator campaigns become possible. The goal is sustainable growth built on relationships, not just transactions.

How Commission Works

You earn first.
We earn after.

Every deal is structured so that the creator is paid before we take anything. No upfront fees. No hidden costs. Our commission is simply a percentage of what you've already received.

This completely aligns our incentives. We can only earn by ensuring you earn — and earn well. If a deal doesn't close, we don't cost you a thing.

Our Guarantees

What you can hold us to.

Identity Never Disclosed Early

Your name, channel, audience size, and location are never shared with any brand until they have confirmed a real budget and passed our values check. No exceptions.

Values-Only Brand Standard

We do not broker deals involving gambling, alcohol, adult content, deceptive financial products, or anything that conflicts with shared values. If we're not sure, we don't pitch it.

15-Day Payout Window

Once we receive payment from a brand, your share is wired within 15 days. No holding funds. No delays. A transparent payment breakdown is provided with every transfer.
