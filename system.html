Our System

A process built to
protect both sides.

Every step is designed with one principle: no one moves forward until both parties are protected. Research-first, contract-backed, and built for the long term. Select your path below.

01
Phase one
Discovery & Briefing

You tell us what winning looks like.

We start with a free 30-minute call. There's no pitch deck, no pressure, and no commitment. We want to understand your product, your audience, and what a successful campaign actually looks like for your business — before we touch anything else.

Brand & product deep-dive

We learn what you sell, who you sell to, what your brand voice is, and what you've tried before. The more context we have, the better the match.

KPI & goal alignment

Are you optimising for awareness, conversions, traffic, or trust-building? We shape the campaign strategy around your specific goal — not a template.

Budget qualification

We establish a budget range before any creator names are discussed. This protects both sides and ensures we only match you with creators whose rates are realistic.

Ethical fit check

We confirm the product and campaign meet our halal ethical standards: no gambling, alcohol, adult content, or misleading claims. This is non-negotiable on every deal.

Creator identity is never disclosed at this stage. We protect our creators' details until budget alignment is confirmed.
02
Phase two
Creator Matching

We find the right creator — not the biggest one.

Using your brief, we search our roster and conduct fresh outreach to identify creators whose audiences genuinely align with your product. Follower count is the last thing we look at.

Engagement audit

We analyse real engagement — saves, shares, comment quality, story interactions. We filter out inflated accounts with passive audiences.

Audience demographic match

We look at age, geography, income signals, and interest clusters to confirm the creator's audience overlaps meaningfully with your target customer.

Content & tone alignment

Does the creator's style, production quality, and voice fit your brand? We review recent content and evaluate natural fit before making any recommendation.

Rate presentation

Once we've confirmed the match, we present the creator profile and rate card. You review and approve before any outreach is made on your behalf.

03
Phase three
Campaign Execution

We run the entire campaign. You stay in control.

Once you've approved the creator, we handle everything: contract drafting, briefing, content review, and approval workflow. You sign off before a single frame goes live.

Contract drafting

We draft a clear agreement covering rate, deliverables, revision rounds, usage rights, content guidelines, and payment timeline. Signed via DocuSign before work starts.

Creator briefing

We send the creator a clean, detailed brief covering brand voice, key messages, dos and don'ts, and the specific deliverable format. No ambiguity.

Content review & approval

All content is reviewed by us before it reaches you. You then give final approval — in writing — before anything is published. Nothing goes live without your sign-off.

Deadline management

We track every milestone and follow up with the creator. You won't need to chase anyone — that's our job from start to finish.

04
Phase four
Delivery & Reporting

Content live. Results tracked. Commission earned.

Once content is published and confirmed, payment is processed and our commission is collected. We provide a clear post-campaign summary and discuss what comes next.

Publication confirmation

We confirm the content is live, matches the approved version, and meets all agreed specifications before any payment is processed.

Payment processing

You pay the creator directly or through Brioo, depending on the agreed structure. Our commission (15–20%) is collected only after payment is confirmed.

Performance summary

We provide a post-campaign summary covering reach, engagement, and any conversion data available. Transparent numbers — the good and the bad.

Relationship building

Great partnerships don't end at one post. We discuss whether a long-term or retainer arrangement with the creator makes sense for your brand.

01
Phase one
Application & Review

You apply. We review every application personally.

There are no automated filters here. When you apply to join the Brioo roster, a real person reviews your content, your audience, and your fit. We'll tell you honestly if we think we can find deals for you — or if the timing isn't right.

Content review

We look at your recent content across your primary platforms — production quality, consistency, authenticity, and how your audience responds to it.

Audience quality check

We review engagement rate, comment quality, follower growth patterns, and geographic breakdown. We're looking for real, engaged people — not inflated numbers.

Niche & values fit

We operate to halal ethical standards. We check that your content is clean, your partnerships have been above board, and that you're aligned with the kinds of brands in our ecosystem.

Decision & feedback

We respond to every application. If the fit is right, we move forward. If not, we'll tell you why and what would change our decision.

You don't need to be Muslim to apply. Our standard is about content ethics, not identity. All backgrounds are welcome.
02
Phase two
Onboarding & Agreement

Non-exclusive. Transparent. Yours to walk away from.

If we're a fit, we send you a non-exclusive creator agreement via DocuSign. We walk you through every clause before you sign. There are no tricks, no lock-ins, and no obligations beyond what's written.

Non-exclusive agreement

You're free to work with other agencies, take independent deals, and build your business however you want. We only ask that you don't work independently with brands we've specifically introduced to you.

Commission structure explained

We earn 15–20% from the brand side only. You never pay us. If we don't close a deal for you, you owe us nothing. Simple and aligned.

Platform & niche profile

We build an internal profile for you covering your platforms, audience demographics, niche, typical deliverables, and rate expectations. This is what we use when pitching brands on your behalf — you approve everything before it's shared.

Rate guidance

We discuss your current rates and what the market supports for your size and niche. We'll push for the number that's fair — not the lowest one a brand will accept.

03
Phase three
Deal Matching & Negotiation

We find the brand. We negotiate the deal. You decide.

When a brand brief comes in that matches your profile, we pitch you as an option — blind, without revealing your identity. Once the brand confirms interest and budget, we present the deal to you. You always have final say.

Blind brand outreach

We pitch your audience demographics and content quality to brands without revealing your name until the brand has confirmed budget alignment. Your identity is protected at every stage.

Rate negotiation

Once a brand is interested, we negotiate your rate using real engagement data. We don't accept the first offer. We push for the number that reflects your actual value.

Deal presentation

We present the full deal to you: the brand, their product, the deliverable, the rate, the timeline, and the usage terms. You review everything before any commitment is made.

Your decision, always

If the deal doesn't feel right — the brand, the rate, the content ask — you decline. No pressure, no explanation required. We move on and find the next one.

04
Phase four
Creation & Payment

You create the content. We handle everything else.

Once you've accepted the deal, a contract is signed and a detailed brief is sent. You focus on making great content. We manage the approval workflow, deadlines, and payment tracking so you're never left chasing anyone.

Contract via DocuSign

Rate, deliverables, revision rounds, usage rights, content ownership, and payment timeline — all locked in writing before a single frame is recorded.

Detailed creative brief

You receive a clean brief with brand guidelines, key messages, and format specifications. Clear enough to protect you, open enough to protect your creative voice.

Approval workflow

We sit between you and the brand for all feedback rounds. You don't deal with the brand directly unless you choose to. We manage revisions, tone, and disputes.

Payment secured

We track payment against the contract timeline. If there's a delay or dispute, we pursue it on your behalf. You receive your share — our commission is taken separately from the brand.

You retain full ownership of all content you create. Usage rights are limited strictly to what's specified in the signed contract.
