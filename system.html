The Brioo System

A playbook built
for scale.

Brioo operates on a five‑phase system: Foundation, Outreach, Deals, Systems, Scale. Every action we take maps to one of these phases.

Five phases.
One outcome.

This isn't a vague "strategy." This is a structured playbook with defined outcomes and clear milestones. Here's how Brioo builds creator businesses — starting from zero.

Phase One01

Foundation

Build everything from scratch: agency identity, website, legal structure, outreach infrastructure, and tracking systems. Nothing moves without this base. Most agencies skip this and wonder why they have no credibility.

Website & BrandingEmail SetupCreator TrackerBrand Pipeline CRMCalendly IntegrationContract Templates
Phase Two02

Outreach

Consistent, high-quality outreach to creators and brands. Every email is short, natural, personal, and inbox-friendly. No bullet points. No AI-sounding copy. Creator identity is never disclosed in brand outreach.

For creators: we sell the pain first — brands are missing this audience, money is being left on the table — then introduce ourselves as the solution.

Personalised emailsBlind pitchingIdentity protectionPipeline tracking
Phase Three03

Deals

When a brand confirms budget interest, we introduce the creator. Negotiation begins. We handle rate discussions, content deliverables, usage rights, timelines, and contract drafting. Creator and brand both sign via DocuSign or PandaDoc.

Payment goes through Brioo or directly to the creator, depending on the deal structure. Our commission (15–20%) is deducted only after the creator is paid. Wire within 15 days of receipt.

Rate negotiationContract via DocuSignPayment tracking15-day payout window
Phase Four04

Systems

Automate what can be automated. Every creator email generates a Google Drive sync. Every brand contact updates the pipeline. Every signed deal triggers a contract folder and payment tracker. We build the backend so we can scale the front end.

Google Drive auto-syncCreator Tracker updatesBrand Pipeline updates
Phase Five05

Scale

Once the first milestone is achieved, we expand the creator roster, establish long-term brand partnerships, and build a reputation as the go-to performance influencer marketing agency. The focus is always on sustainable growth and genuine relationships.

Roster expansionRetainer brand dealsMulti-creator campaignsSustainable growth

How Commission Works

You earn first.
We earn after.

Every deal is structured so that the creator is paid before we take anything. No upfront fees. No hidden costs. Our commission is simply a percentage of what you've already received.

This aligns our incentives completely. We can only earn by ensuring you earn — and earn well.
