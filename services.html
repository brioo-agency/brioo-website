Home — Services

Everything between a creator and a paid partnership.

Four services, one standard. We represent the creator, court the brand, write the contract, and handle the money — so every partnership is built on trust and backed in writing. Here's exactly what that looks like, for both sides.

01 — Representation 02 — Outreach 03 — Contracts 04 — Payments
01

Creator Representation

We become the business behind your brand.

You built an audience that trusts you — and that trust has real commercial value. We turn it into consistent, well-negotiated work, while your name only reaches the right rooms, on your terms, and never before you're ready. Non-exclusive, always. You're represented, not owned.

For creators

  • Pitched anonymously — your identity stays private until budget is real.
  • Inbound opportunities matched to your audience and your rate.
  • A team that negotiates for you — never against you.
  • Non-exclusive representation — you're never locked in.

For brands

  • Access to creators with genuinely engaged audiences.
  • Every creator pre-screened for alignment before any introduction.
  • One accountable point of contact, start to finish.
  • Relationships built for the long term, not a one-off post.

You stay focused on the work. We handle the room.

02

Outreach & Negotiation

This is where we find you deals worth taking.

We approach brands that genuinely fit your audience, lead with your numbers, and negotiate every term until it's fair. No spray-and-pray pitching, no vanity metrics — just well-matched partnerships, priced on real performance. You approve every brand before a conversation begins.

For creators

  • We find and pitch brands that fit — you approve every one.
  • Rates negotiated on data, not guesswork.
  • Deliverables, usage, exclusivity and timelines argued in your favour.
  • Nothing moves forward without your sign-off.

For brands

  • Proposals grounded in real engagement and demographics.
  • Clear scope and pricing before anyone commits.
  • A partner who protects the relationship for the long term.
  • The right creator, not just an available one.

Brands don't pay for reach. They pay for trust — and we price it properly.

03

Contracts & Legal

Every partnership is contract-backed.

Most disputes trace back to what was never written down — scope creep, late payment, unclear usage. We prevent all of it. The terms are clear, the protections mutual, and nothing is left to assumption. Both sides sign before a single frame of content is made.

For creators

  • IP and usage rights defined before you create.
  • Payment milestones and deadlines in writing.
  • Revision limits, so scope can't quietly creep.
  • You always know what you owe and what you're owed.

For brands

  • Deliverables and rights you can rely on.
  • Clear approvals, revisions, and timelines.
  • A professional standard upheld on both sides.
  • Compliance and disclosure handled correctly.

If it isn't written down, it isn't a partnership.

04

Commission-Only Payments

We only earn after you do.

Most agencies charge retainers or setup fees regardless of results. We're built the opposite way — our 15–20% comes out of money already in your account, never before. Our incentives sit exactly on top of yours, so we win only when the partnership does.

For creators

  • Zero upfront, zero risk — no deal, no cost.
  • A transparent breakdown on every single transfer.
  • Paid promptly once the brand pays.
  • You always keep the larger share.

For brands

  • No agency retainer inflating your budget.
  • Incentives aligned with real performance.
  • One clean, fully-accounted invoice.
  • Budget that goes to the work, not overhead.

No retainers. No setup fees. No payment until you've been paid.
