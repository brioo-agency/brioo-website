Common questions

Straight answers, no fluff.

How do you vet creators?+
We personally review engagement quality, audience demographics, comment authenticity, and past brand collaborations. No bots, no shortcuts.
What niches do you specialize in?+
Luxury, lifestyle, fitness, finance, self‑improvement, and ethical e‑commerce. All campaigns meet our integrity standards.
Do you work with brands outside certain backgrounds?+
Absolutely. We partner with any brand that aligns with professional ethics – no gambling, alcohol, or deceptive products.
What's the typical timeline?+
From briefing to campaign launch: 2‑4 weeks. We move fast but never skip the relationship‑building phase.
How much commission do you take?+
Only 15-20% – and only when you get paid. No hidden fees, no retainers.
Do I need a huge following?+
No. We care about engagement rate and audience trust. Micro‑creators often outperform mega‑influencers.
What kind of brands will I work with?+
Premium, values‑aligned brands that respect creative freedom. No shady deals.
How do I apply?+
Email creators@brioo.org with your social handles and engagement screenshots – we reply within 72h.
