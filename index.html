Questions answered

Everything you need
to know.

Got a question not answered here? Drop us a message — we reply within 24 hours.

How does Brioo find the right creator for my brand?+

We don't guess. Every match is preceded by a manual audit covering engagement rate, audience demographics, content quality, niche alignment, and follower authenticity. We only present creators whose audiences genuinely overlap with your target market — not just whoever has the biggest numbers.

How does the commission model work?+

Brioo charges 15–20% commission, taken only after the creator has delivered content and you've paid them. There are no upfront agency fees, no retainers, and no charges before you've seen results. Our incentive is perfectly aligned with yours: we only earn when the deal closes.

Can I review the content before it goes live?+

Yes, always. Content review and approval is built into every campaign. Nothing gets published without your written sign-off. We also ensure the creator understands your brand guidelines before a single frame is recorded.

What niches does Brioo work in?+

We operate to halal standards on every partnership — meaning no gambling, alcohol, adult content, or misleading claims. This isn't a religious filter; it's an ethical standard. Our brands and creators come from all backgrounds. What matters is that the content is clean, the deal is honest, and the values are aligned. We work across education, family, wellness, finance, lifestyle, business, and personal development.

Do your creators need to be Muslim?+

No. Our creators come from a wide range of backgrounds. The standard we apply is about content and ethics, not identity. If a creator produces clean, values-aligned content with a genuinely engaged audience, we want to work with them — regardless of their background.

When will you reveal who the creator is?+

Creator identity is shared only after a call where we've confirmed your budget fits the creator's rate. This protects the creator's privacy during the exploratory phase and ensures we're not wasting your time with creators whose rates don't match your budget.

How long does a campaign take from start to finish?+

Most campaigns move from signed agreement to published content within 2–4 weeks. The timeline depends on the creator's schedule and agreed deliverables, and is always confirmed in writing before any work begins. We stay on top of deadlines so you don't have to.

How much does it cost to work with Brioo?+

Nothing. Zero. Joining the Brioo roster is completely free. We take our 15–20% commission from the brand-side payment, not from you. You never pay us anything directly. If we don't close a deal for you, you owe us nothing.

Do I have to be exclusive to Brioo?+

No. Our creator agreement is non-exclusive. You're free to work with other agencies, negotiate your own deals, and build your business however you see fit. The only restriction is that you cannot independently work with brands that Brioo specifically introduced to you — which is standard practice across the industry.

What size do I need to be to join?+

We look at engagement quality over follower count. If your audience is genuinely engaged, your content is values-aligned, and you're serious about professional partnerships — we want to talk. We work with a range of creator sizes and evaluate each application individually.

How and when do I get paid?+

Payment terms are agreed and written into the contract before any work begins. You receive your share after the brand completes their payment following content delivery. Brioo manages the payment process and follows up on any delays on your behalf.

Do I need to be Muslim to join?+

No. Brioo's standard is about content ethics, not religion. We operate to halal principles — no gambling, alcohol, adult content, or deceptive claims — because we believe ethical partnerships produce better results for everyone. Our creators come from all backgrounds. What we care about is clean content and a genuine audience.

What if I don't like the brand brief?+

You always have the final say. We present deals for your review before any commitment is made. If the brand isn't the right fit, the rate is too low, or the content ask doesn't align with your values — you simply decline. We will never push you into a partnership that doesn't feel right.

Do I retain ownership of my content?+

Yes. You retain full ownership of everything you create. The contract specifies the exact usage rights granted to the brand for the duration agreed — and nothing beyond that. Any extended usage or repurposing requires your separate consent.
