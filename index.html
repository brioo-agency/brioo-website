What We Do

Everything between a creator
and a paid deal.

From brand identification to signed contracts and payment collection. Four core services that cover the full pipeline.

Creator Representation

We represent you to brands — anonymously until budget is confirmed. No cold outreach on your end. Just inbound deals that match your audience and your terms.

You've built an audience that trusts you. That has real value — and brands know it. But most creators don't have the time, contacts, or negotiation experience to turn that trust into consistent income. We bridge that gap.

We pitch you entirely anonymously. Your name, channel, and location stay hidden until a brand confirms real budget. We then negotiate rates, deliverables, and usage rights on your behalf before anything is signed.

Brand Outreach & Negotiation

We identify brands that align with your audience, pitch them on your engagement and demographics, and negotiate every term — rates, deliverables, usage rights, and timelines.

We don't spray generic pitches. Every brand we approach is researched for alignment with your content and audience. We lead with data — engagement rates, audience demographics, past performance — not vanity metrics.

Once a brand shows interest and confirms budget, we handle the full negotiation: deliverables, usage rights, exclusivity windows, revision limits, and payment terms. You review and approve before anything is signed.

Contracts & Legal Management

Binding contracts through secure digital agreement. IP protection, usage rights, and payment terms locked in before you create a single piece of content.

Most creator disputes come from what wasn't written down. Scope creep, delayed payment, unclear usage rights — these problems are preventable with proper contracts from the start.

Every Brioo deal uses secure digital contracting. IP ownership, revision limits, payment milestones, and deadlines are all defined upfront. Both parties sign before any content is produced. Clear, fair, enforceable.

Commission-Only Payments

We earn when you earn — after you're paid. Our 15–20% is deducted and the remainder transferred within 15 days. Zero upfront investment. Zero risk.

Most agencies charge retainers, setup fees, or monthly management costs — regardless of whether they close a single deal. Brioo is structured differently by design.

We only earn when you do. After the brand pays and you've received your share, we deduct our 15–20% commission. Payouts are completed within 15 days of receipt. Transparent breakdown provided with every transfer. If we don't close a deal, it costs you nothing.

Built to fix a broken system.

Creators with loyal, values-driven audiences were getting ignored by brands who couldn't find them. Meanwhile, those same brands were wasting budgets on influencers who didn't convert. Our Commitment

Ethics
Statement

Effective May 29, 2026. At Brioo, ethical conduct isn't a marketing position — it is the foundation every single decision is built on.

What We Stand For

Six principles we publicly commit to — and hold ourselves accountable to.

01

Radical Honesty

We never fabricate follower counts, engagement metrics, or case studies. We don't use bots or fake accounts. We are transparent about our size, our process, and our results — even when they are imperfect. A creator deserves an honest agency.

02

Fair Compensation, Always

We never ask creators to work "for exposure." Every campaign includes a paid contract with clear terms. We negotiate on behalf of creators to ensure they receive fair market rates — and we only take our cut after they receive theirs.

03

No Harmful Industries

Gambling, alcohol, adult content, weapons, interest-based finance, deceptive products — all rejected without exception. We will turn down commission rather than broker a deal that could harm our creators' audience or violate our ethical standards.

04

Transparency with Data

We provide honest reporting to brands — including real engagement rates, reach, clicks, and conversions. We do not hide underperformance. We use the data to improve, never to mislead. Truth earns more trust than spin ever will.

05

Long-Term Over Short-Term

We prioritise trust over commissions. If a deal does not genuinely benefit both the creator and the brand, we will not facilitate it. We believe that ethical business compounds over time — and unsustainable shortcuts cost more than they're worth.

06

Full Accountability

If we make a mistake, we own it. If a campaign underperforms, we analyse why and adjust our process. We welcome feedback from all partners. We do not hide behind fine print or deflect responsibility. This statement is a public commitment we invite you to hold us to.

Brioo Agency ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website (brioo.org) or services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: your name, email address, social media handles, platform analytics and engagement data, and campaign preferences. We may also collect basic usage data via cookies — such as pages visited and referral source — to understand how our website is being used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: match creators with appropriate brand opportunities; communicate with you about campaigns and opportunities; process payments; improve our services over time; and comply with applicable legal obligations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties — ever.

3. Sharing of Information

We share necessary information with brands and creators only to facilitate active campaigns. Critically: we never share a creator's identity with a brand until the brand has confirmed genuine budget and interest on a call. We may share anonymised, aggregate data for general marketing purposes. We use Stripe or PayPal for payment processing and Calendly for scheduling — both have their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including encrypted email, secure storage, and access controls. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. By using our services, you acknowledge this inherent risk.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing contact@brioo.org. We will respond within 30 days. If we are unable to fulfil your request, we will explain why.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate the site correctly. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though some site functionality may be affected.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately at contact@brioo.org.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify active users by email of any material changes. Continued use of our services after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
