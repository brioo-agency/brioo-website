Why Brioo

Built on process,
not promises.

Three principles that govern every deal — for every creator, every brand, no exceptions.

Your identity is safe

We never reveal your name, location, audience size, or channel to a brand until they confirm a real budget. You control when and how your identity is shared — always.

Vetted brands only

We turn down gambling, alcohol, adult content, deceptive financial products, and anything that clashes with professional standards. Every brand is reviewed before we pitch a creator.

We earn only after you do

No retainers. No setup fees. No payment until the deal closes and you've been paid. Our 15–20% comes out of what you've already received — never charged upfront.

No Upfront Fees

No retainers, no setup costs. If we don't close a deal, it costs you nothing.

No Hidden Cuts

Our structure is transparent from day one. You know exactly what you'll receive before signing.

No Unapproved Sharing

Your information is never disclosed without explicit permission. Full stop.

No Pressure, Ever

Every opportunity is optional. We advise on fit and value. You decide — always.
