What We Do

Everything between
a creator and a paid deal.

Four core services covering the complete pipeline — from brand identification to signed contracts and payment collection.

Creator Representation

We represent you to brands — anonymously until budget is confirmed. No cold outreach. No pitching. Just inbound deals that match your audience and your terms.

You've built an audience that trusts you. That trust has real commercial value — and brands know it. But most creators lack the time, contacts, or negotiation experience to convert that trust into consistent income.

We pitch you entirely anonymously. Your name, channel, and location remain hidden until a brand confirms real budget. We then negotiate rates, deliverables, and usage rights on your behalf before anything is signed.

Brand Outreach & Negotiation

We identify aligned brands, pitch them on your engagement and demographics, and negotiate every term — rates, deliverables, usage rights, exclusivity, and timelines.

We don't spray generic pitches. Every brand we approach is researched for audience alignment. We lead with data — engagement rates, demographics, content performance — not vanity metrics.

Once a brand confirms budget, we handle full negotiation: deliverables, usage rights, exclusivity windows, revision limits, and payment terms. You review and approve before anything is signed.

Contracts & Legal Management

Binding digital contracts with IP protection, usage rights, and payment terms locked in before you create a single piece of content.

Most creator disputes come from what wasn't written down. Scope creep, delayed payment, unclear usage rights — preventable with proper contracts from the start.

Every Brioo deal uses secure digital contracting. IP ownership, revision limits, payment milestones, and deadlines are defined upfront. Both parties sign before any content is produced.

Commission-Only Payments

We earn when you earn — after you're paid. Our 15–20% is deducted from what you've already received. Zero upfront. Zero risk. No deal, no cost.

Most agencies charge retainers or setup fees regardless of results. Brioo is structured differently by design. Our incentives are completely aligned with yours.

After the brand pays and you've received your share, we deduct our commission. Payouts completed within 15 days. Transparent breakdown with every transfer. If we don't close a deal, it costs you nothing.

Three principles that define how we operate — for every creator, every brand, every deal.

We never share your name, location, audience size, or channel with any brand until they have confirmed a real budget. You control when and how your identity is revealed.

We reject gambling, alcohol, adult content, deceptive financial products, and anything misaligned with professional standards. Every brand is reviewed before we pitch any creator.

We Only Earn After You Do
