Creator Representation

We pitch you to brands anonymously — your identity protected until budget is confirmed. No cold outreach on your end. Just inbound deals that match who you are.

You've built an audience that trusts you — that's rare, and brands know it. The problem is most of them have no idea how to find you, approach you, or offer something fair. We change that.

We pitch you entirely anonymously. Your name, channel, and location stay hidden until a brand confirms real budget. No unsolicited DMs, no lowball offers. Just deals that match your values.

Brand Outreach & Negotiation

We find brands aligned with your audience, pitch them on your ROI, and negotiate every term on your behalf — rates, deliverables, usage rights, and timelines.

niche audiences are among the most brand-loyal communities online — yet most agencies have no idea how to reach them. Brioo does.

We identify genuinely aligned brands, pitch them on ROI and audience demographics — not vanity metrics — and negotiate every term so the deal is fair from both sides.

Contracts & Legal Management

Binding contracts via DocuSign. IP protection, usage rights, and payment terms settled before you create a single piece of content. Clear, fair, enforceable.

Most creator deals fall apart because of what wasn't in writing. Scope creep, delayed payment, unclear usage rights — these are the problems that eat into what you earned.

Every Brioo deal is backed by a binding contract via DocuSign. IP ownership, revision limits, payment terms, and deadlines are all locked in before any content is created.

Commission-Only Payments

We earn when you earn — after you're paid. Our 15–20% is deducted and the remainder transferred within 15 days. Zero risk. Zero upfront investment.

Every other agency asks you to pay before you see results. Retainers, setup fees, management fees — costs that exist whether or not a deal closes. Brioo is different by design.

We earn only when you earn. After you're paid, we deduct our 15–20% and wire the remainder within 15 days. If a deal doesn't close, we don't cost you anything.
