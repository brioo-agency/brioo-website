Why Brioo

Built on process,
not promises.

Three principles that govern every deal — for every creator, every brand, no exceptions.

Your identity is safe

We never reveal your name, location, audience size, or channel to a brand until they confirm a real budget. You control when and how it's shared.

Vetted brands only

We turn down gambling, alcohol, adult content, deceptive financial products, and anything that clashes with professional standards.

We earn only after you do

No retainers, no setup fees. Our 15–20% comes out of what you've already received — never charged upfront.
