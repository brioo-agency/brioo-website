Ethics Statement

Effective: May 29, 2026

At Brioo, ethical conduct is not a marketing gimmick — it is the foundation of everything we do. This statement publicly commits us to the following principles.

1. Honesty

We never fabricate follower counts, engagement metrics, or case studies. We do not use bots or fake accounts. We are transparent about our size, our process, and our results — even when they are not perfect.

2. Fair Compensation

We never ask creators to work "for exposure." Every campaign includes a paid contract with clear terms. We negotiate on behalf of creators to ensure they receive fair market rates.

3. No Harmful Industries

We refuse to work with gambling, alcohol, adult content, weapons, or any product that is deceptive or harms vulnerable people. Brands in these categories will be rejected without exception.

4. Transparency with Data

We provide honest reporting to brands — including engagement rates, reach, clicks, and conversions. We do not hide underperformance. We use the data to improve, not to mislead.

5. Long‑term Relationships

We prioritize trust over short‑term commissions. If a deal does not benefit both the brand and the creator, we will not facilitate it. We believe that ethical business is sustainable business.

6. Accountability

If we make a mistake, we own it. If a campaign underperforms, we analyze why and adjust our process. We welcome feedback from all partners.

For any ethics concerns, please contact ethics@brioo.org.
