Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Brioo Agency ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or services.

We collect information you provide directly: name, email address, social media handles, audience engagement data, and campaign preferences. We also collect usage data via cookies (e.g., pages visited, referral source).

2. How We Use Your Information

To match brands with creators, communicate about campaigns, process payments, improve our services, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Sharing of Information

We share necessary information with brands and creators only to facilitate campaigns. We may share anonymized aggregate data for marketing purposes. We use trusted payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) and Calendly for scheduling.

4. Data Security

We implement industry‑standard security measures. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You use our services at your own risk.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by emailing privacy@brioo.org. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate the site. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
