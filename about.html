Why we exist

The system was broken.
So we built a new one.

Too many creators with genuinely engaged audiences never land brand deals because the system rewards follower count over real influence. Too many brands burn budgets on campaigns that look good on a spreadsheet and do nothing in the real world.

Brioo was built to fix that — not with hype, not with inflated promises, but with a simple, repeatable process anchored in research, honesty, and long-term thinking.

We operate to halal standards on every deal. That means no gambling, alcohol, adult content, or deceptive claims. You don't need to be Muslim to work with us. You just need to care about clean, ethical partnerships — and so do we.

What guides us

Three principles.
No compromises.

⚡

Honesty

We say what we can do — and we don't say what we can't. No overpromising, no fake metrics, no inflated reach numbers to close a deal. If the fit isn't right, we'll tell you.

📆

Consistency

Daily outreach, clear follow-ups, and communication that doesn't disappear. Every creator and every brand gets the same level of attention — all the time.

💎

Value First

If a deal doesn't benefit both sides, we don't make it. Short-term commissions are worthless if the partnership damages trust — on either side.

How we're different

Not a typical agency.

01
Commission-only model
You pay 15–20% only after the creator delivers and you've paid. No retainer, no upfront cost. If we don't perform, you don't pay us anything.
02
Creator identity protected until you're ready
We qualify your budget and goals on a call before revealing any creator. No fishing for info, no pressure — just a clean, professional process.
03
Ethical standards on every deal
Every partnership meets halal ethical standards: no gambling, alcohol, adult content, or misleading claims. Brands in our ecosystem reach audiences built on trust.
04
Radical transparency
You see exactly what was researched, who was contacted, what was offered, and what the results were. No black-box reporting. No vanishing after signing.
01
Zero cost — ever
Joining Brioo costs nothing. We take our commission from the brand side only. You never pay us a single dollar, even if a deal doesn't close.
02
Non-exclusive by design
We don't lock you in. You're free to take independent deals, work with other agencies, and run your business however you want. We earn by adding value, not by owning you.
03
We fight for your rate
We negotiate your deal with real engagement data behind us. No underselling, no accepting lowball offers, no pressure to take a deal that doesn't serve you.
04
Contract on every single deal
Your rate, usage rights, revision limits, and payment timeline are locked in writing before any work starts. Disputes are handled by us — so you never have to chase anyone.
