About Brioo

Built for creators
who value trust.

Brioo wasn't created to be another marketing agency. It was created because no one was doing this properly — and creators were leaving real money on the table.

Our Origin

The gap was obvious.
Nobody was filling it.

Creators — from educators to entertainers — were building massive, loyal audiences. Brands worth working with had no idea how to reach them. Agencies didn't understand the niche, the values, or why certain partnerships simply don't work.

"If you wouldn't recommend it to your own audience, we won't pitch it to theirs."
— The Brioo Standard

So we built Brioo: a commission-only agency that sits at the intersection of the creator economy and brands that deserve access to it. No upfront costs. No compromises. Just performance.

What We Stand For

Three principles we
never negotiate on.

01

Honesty First

We never fabricate metrics, inflate follower counts, or misrepresent a deal. If a partnership isn't right for you, we say so — even if it costs us a commission. Your trust is worth more than any deal.

02

Consistency Always

We show up for every creator the same way, regardless of follower count. A creator at 200K gets the same diligence and care as one at 2M. We do our best and trust the outcome.

03

Value Before Commission

We only pursue deals that genuinely benefit you. If the terms aren't fair, we walk away. Our 15–20% only means something if you're getting 80–85% of something real. We earn by delivering value — not chasing it.
