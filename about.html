About Brioo

Built to fix a
broken system.

Creators with loyal, values-driven audiences were getting ignored by brands who couldn't find them. Meanwhile, those same brands were wasting budgets on influencers who didn't convert. Brioo was built to close that gap — connecting the right creators with the right brands, with no upfront cost and no compromises on values.

Who We Built This For

Two groups leaving
money on the table.

For Creators

You've spent years building trust. Where are the deals?

"I know my audience is loyal. I know brands want them. But I have no idea how to connect the two — and I don't have time to figure it out."

Most creators with highly engaged, trust-based audiences are significantly undermonetised. The problem isn't demand — it's that no one is brokering the connection properly. Brioo is that bridge. We find the brands, protect your identity until budget is confirmed, negotiate your rates, and handle everything through to payment. You stay focused on what you do best: creating.

For Brands

Your customer is already following someone. You just can't reach them.

"We've spent on influencer campaigns before and seen almost no return. The creators didn't actually connect with our audience — or our values."

Audiences built on trust convert at rates that broad-reach influencers can't match. But reaching them requires more than a follower count — it requires genuine alignment between brand, creator, and community. Brioo identifies the creators whose audiences already match your customer profile, pitches them blind to protect their identity, and only makes the introduction once real budget is on the table. You pay when the deal closes — not before.

Our Origin

The gap was obvious.
Nobody was filling it.

Creators in values-driven spaces — educators, family content creators, community builders, faith-aligned voices — were growing some of the most loyal audiences on the internet. Brands with real budgets had no structured way to reach them. Existing agencies either didn't understand the communities, pushed creators into misaligned deals for quick commissions, or ignored these niches entirely because they weren't mass-market enough.

The result? Creators leaving significant income on the table. Brands spending blindly on influencers who couldn't convert their actual target audience. And an entire segment of the creator economy being served by nobody.

"If you wouldn't recommend it to your own audience, we won't pitch it to theirs."
— The Brioo Standard

So we built Brioo: a commission-only agency that sits at the intersection of trusted creators and brands that deserve access to their audiences. No upfront costs. No compromises on values. Just performance — and a promise that every deal serves both sides.

What We Stand For

Three principles we
never negotiate on.

01

Honesty First

We never fabricate metrics, inflate numbers, or misrepresent a deal. If a partnership isn't right for you, we say so — even if it costs us a commission. Your reputation is worth more than any single payday, and we protect it like our own.

02

Consistency Always

Every creator gets the same standard of care, whether they have 20K followers or 2 million. The diligence, the negotiation, the follow-through — none of it changes based on size. We do the work properly and let the results speak.

03

Value Before Commission

We only pursue deals that genuinely benefit you. If the terms aren't fair, we walk. Our 15–20% commission only means something if you're getting 80–85% of something real. We earn by delivering value — not by chasing every offer that comes through the door.

The Founder

Founder & CEO

Abdiqani Moallin

Founder — Brioo Agency

Abdiqani built Brioo after watching the same pattern repeat across the creator economy: creators with deeply loyal, values-aligned audiences had no structured way to turn that trust into brand income — and brands who wanted exactly those audiences couldn't find them.

With a background in digital operations and systems building, Abdiqani launched Brioo as a commission-only operation from day one. No overheads passed to creators. No upfront fees. No deals that don't pass a values check. The model was designed so that Brioo only succeeds when creators and brands both win — and win fairly.

The agency exists to serve its partners, not the other way around. Every decision — from which brands to approach to how contracts are structured — is measured by a single question: does this build trust?
